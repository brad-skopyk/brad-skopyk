Digital History Interests

Highlights:

  • Working on a geolocated historical database of health & ecology in colonial Mexico —ecocrisis.net. The database has about 10k geolocated historical events. We are looking for historians and coders to help with the project.
  • Working on a geolocated database of historical demography in Mexico
  • Working on integrating data and cartographic visualizations using R and Python into a web interface
  • Working on understanding the impact of sixteenth- and seventeenth-century climate fluctuations on human food systems and health
  • Working on history of water, especially at the intersection of climate, religion, and hydraulic projects in colonial Mexico
